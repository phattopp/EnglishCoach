Sommercamps

Unsere Mission

Neue Abenteuer erleben

At our camps, children are encouraged to step out of their comfort zones, try new activities, and build confidence while discovering their own strengths. They take part in a wide range of fun outdoor games and adventurous sports—many of which they may be experiencing for the very first time. Teamwork, creativity, and a sense of adventure are at the heart of everything we do.

We offer day camps which take place down at a lake with the main activity being SUP tours around the lake every day. We also offer a camp which runs over a full week in the beautiful Mölltal region of Carinthia where the children sleep in Canadian styles log cabins with access to a communal hut for indoors and group activities. These 2 options give families flexible options to suit their needs during the summer months and wether children join us for the day camps or stay for the full week experience, they enjoy a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment.

English learning is woven into every part of the camp experience. Through a combination of age and curriculum appropriate classroom sessions and plenty of outdoor activities, children practice speaking, expand their vocabulary, and improve their grammar. By using English throughout the day—in games, conversations, and challenges—they become more confident, fluent, and comfortable using the language in real-life situations.
